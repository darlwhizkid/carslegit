Frequently Asked Questions

How can I track my application status

Every application receives a unique tracking number. Simply log into your CarLegit dashboard to check real-time updates on your application status.

How long does vehicle registration take?

With CarLegit, new vehicle registration typically takes 24-48 hours to process. Our digital system streamlines the entire process, making it significantly faster than traditional methods.

What documents do I need for ownership transfer

You'll need the original vehicle title, valid ID, proof of insurance, and completed transfer forms. Our system will guide you through each required document and help you prepare everything correctly.

Can I renew my driver's license online?

Yes! Driver's license renewal can be completed entirely online through our platform. The process is secure, quick, and you'll receive step-by-step guidance throughout.
